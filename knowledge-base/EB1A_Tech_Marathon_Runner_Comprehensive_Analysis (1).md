## SECTION 2: CRITERION-BY-CRITERION ANALYSIS

### EB1A-CRITERION 1: AWARDS & PRIZES

#### A. Regulatory Requirement

**8 CFR § 204.5(h)(3)(i):** "Documentation of the beneficiary's receipt of lesser nationally or internationally recognized prizes or awards for excellence in the field of endeavor."

The regulation requires:
1. **Nationally or Internationally Recognized**: Awards must be recognized beyond local/regional scope
2. **For Excellence**: Awards must be given for achievement/excellence, not participation
3. **In the Field**: Awards must relate to beneficiary's field of endeavor (athletics/marathon running)
4. **"Lesser"**: Distinguished from major international awards (Olympics, World Championships), but still significant

**USCIS Policy Manual (Volume 6, Part F, Chapter 2)** clarifies that awards must be recognized in the field and given for excellence rather than participation.

*Media and Industry Recognition:* The B.A.A. qualification standards are widely reported in major sports media (ESPN, Sports Illustrated, Runner's World) and are considered the definitive benchmark for marathon performance.
