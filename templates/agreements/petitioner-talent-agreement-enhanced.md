## SECTION G: AMENDMENT AND TERMINATION

### G.1 Amendment Process
This Agreement may be amended by:
- Mutual written agreement of all parties
- Without consent of any non-party
- With notification to USCIS if amendment affects petition basis

### G.2 Termination Events
The Engagement Period shall terminate upon the earliest of:
1. Expiration of the approved visa validity period
2. Termination of the Beneficiary's right to be employed in the United States
3. Termination of the Engagement by either party (at-will relationship)
4. Revocation of the approved petition by USCIS
5. Mutual written agreement to terminate

### G.3 Post-Termination Obligations
Upon termination:
- Petitioner will notify USCIS within 30 days if termination is prior to visa expiration
- Petitioner will secure reasonable return travel for Beneficiary if visa is terminated early
- All outstanding compensation will be paid within 30 days
