## SECTION 5: COMPLIANCE AND OBLIGATIONS

### 5.1 Beneficiary Obligations

The Beneficiary agrees to:

1. Maintain valid immigration status at all times
2. Only engage in activities consistent with visa classification
3. Notify Agent of any material changes to employment or activities
4. Maintain accurate records of all engagements and income
5. Comply with all U.S. tax obligations
6. Not engage in unauthorized employment outside the scope of this agreement
7. Provide regular activity reports to Agent as requested

### 5.2 Agent/Petitioner Obligations

The Agent/Petitioner agrees to:

1. File the visa petition and any required amendments
2. Accept service of process on behalf of Beneficiary
3. Monitor compliance with immigration regulations
4. Coordinate and approve engagements within visa scope
5. Maintain records of Beneficiary's activities
6. Notify USCIS of any material changes as required
7. Assist with visa extension filings as needed
8. Secure reasonable return transportation if visa is terminated early

### 5.3 Foreign Employer Obligations (If Applicable)

The Foreign Employer agrees to:

1. Authorize the Agent to file the petition
2. Confirm continuing employment relationship (if applicable)
3. Provide necessary documentation for the petition
4. Cooperate with any USCIS requests for information

## SECTION 8: TERMINATION

### 8.1 Termination Events

This Agreement shall terminate upon the earliest of:

1. Expiration of the Beneficiary's visa status
2. Denial or revocation of the visa petition
3. Mutual written agreement of the parties
4. Material breach by any party (with 30 days written notice to cure)
5. Death or incapacity of the Beneficiary

### 8.2 Effect of Termination

Upon termination:
- Agent will assist with orderly wind-down of U.S. activities
- Beneficiary will depart the U.S. in compliance with immigration law
- Final accounting of all financial matters will be completed within 60 days
